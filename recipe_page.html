Hearty Filled Chicken Fillet Delight

Looking for a comforting meal that’s packed with flavor and sure to impress? This Hearty Filled Chicken Fillet Delight is the perfect choice. Tender chicken breasts are generously filled with a savory blend of ingredients, creating a deliciously satisfying dish that’s both wholesome and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Fillets
  • Couscous
  • Baked Beans
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Jimmy's Sauce
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven/airfryer to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Line a baking sheet or air fryer tray with parchment paper and lightly brush it with oil before placing it into the oven or air fryer.
  3. Season the chicken fillets generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper on both sides.
  4. Place the chicken fillets into the oven or air fryer and cook for 9 minutes. Then, flip them over and continue cooking for another 9 minutes, until golden and fully cooked through.
  5. Flip the chicken fillets once more and generously brush Jimmy’s sauce onto one side. Return to the oven or air fryer and cook for an additional 4 minutes to allow the sauce to glaze and caramelize slightly.
  6. While the sauce is glazing, boil water in a kettle to prepare for your side dish.
  7. Once the 4 minutes are up, flip the chicken fillets again, brush Jimmy’s sauce onto the other side, and cook for an additional 4 minutes until beautifully glazed.
  8. Add the couscous to a heatproof bowl, then season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Pour in just enough boiling water to cover the couscous, with a thin layer of water sitting above the grains. Give it a quick stir, then cover the bowl and let it steam until the couscous is tender and fluffy.
  9. Once the couscous and chicken is done, open a can of baked beans and spoon your desired amount onto a plate alongside the chicken and couscous.
  10. Finally, sit back, relax, and enjoy your meal with your favorite beverage of choice.
